32. If the complex number z satisfies the equation z(2 - 3i) = 6 + 4i (where i is the imaginary unit), then the modulus of z is ...​


[tex]z(2-3i) = 6 + 4i[/tex]

kalikan kedua ruas dengan konjugat dari [tex](2-3i)[/tex] yaitu [tex]2+3i[/tex]

[tex]z(2-3i)(2+3i) = (6+4i)(2+3i)\\ z(4+6i-6i-9i^2) = 12+18i+8i+12i^2\\ z(4+9) = 12+26i - 12\\ z(13) = 26i\\ z = 2i[/tex]

maka modulusnya adalah

[tex]|z| = \sqrt{0^2 + 2^2} = 2[/tex]
