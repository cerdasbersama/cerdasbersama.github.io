Susunannya adalah sebagai berikut:How long Earth will it take to orbit around the sun?Earth will take 365 day to orbit it around the sun.How long Mars will it take to orbit around the sun?Mars will take less time to orbit it around the sun.

PEMBAHASANFuture Tense

Digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian yang akan terjadi nanti. Rumusnya:

(+) S + will + V1 + O/C(-) S + will not + V1 + O/C(?) WH + will + S + V1 + O/C?

Maka berikut:

1. How long - to orbit - around the sun - will it take - ? - Earth

➡️ How long Earth will it take to orbit around the sun?

Artinya: Berapa lama waktu yang dibutuhkan bumi untuk mengorbit mengelilingi matahari?

Keterangan:

WH question = How long EarthWill + S + V1 = will it takeTo infinitve ➡️ to+ V1 = to orbitComplement = around the sun

2. will take - to orbit - 365 day - it - Earth - around the sun.

➡️ Earth will take 365 day to orbit it around the sun.

Artinya: Bumi akan membutuhkan 365 hari untuk mengorbit mengelilingi matahari.

Keterangan:

Subject = EarthWill + V1 = will takeObject = 365 dayTo infinitve ➡️ to + V1 = to orbitComplement = around the sun

3. will it take - ? - to orbit - How long - the sun - Mars - around

➡️ How long Mars will it take to orbit around the sun?

Artinya; Berapa lama Mars untuk mengorbit mengelilingi matahari?

Keterangan:

WH question = How long MarsWill + S + V1 = will it takeTo infinitve ➡️ to + V1 = to orbitComplement = around the sun

4. it will take - the sun. - around - less time - to orbit - Mars

➡️ Mars will take less time to orbit it around the sun.

Artinya: Mars akan membutuhkan lebih sedikit waktu untuk mengorbit mengelilingi matahari.

Keterangan:

Subject = MarsWill + V1 = will take To infinitve ➡️ to + V1 = to orbitComplement = around the sun
