23. Ubahlah kalimat active menjadi passive 1. I will create a new on content on my youtube Channel 2. She makes fresh cookies for her son everyday 3. They are waiting for a new movie 4. My sister has posted her pictures on instagram 5. I followed his twitter last month


Jawaban:

||ACTIVE VOICE → PASSIVE VOICE ||

1. I will create a new content on my youtube Channel. (simple future tense)

Passive → A new content will be created by me on my youtube channel.

2. She makes fresh cookies for her son everyday. (simple present tense)

Passive → Fresh cookies are made by her for her son everyday.

3. They are waiting for a new movie. (present continuous tense)

Passive → A new movie are being waited for by them.

4. My sister has posted her pictures on instagram. (present perfect tense)

→ The pictures has been posted by my sister on instagram.

5. I followed his twitter last month. (simple past tense)

Passive → His twitter was followed by me last month.

Penjelasan:

||RUMUS MENGUBAH ACTIVE VOICE → PASSIVE VOICE ||

A. Simple Present Tense

Active : S + verb-1 + O

Passive : S + to be (is/am/are) + verb-3 + by + O

Contoh :

Active : She writes a letter.Passive : A letter is written by her.

B. Simple Past Tense

Active : S + verb-2 + O

Passive : S + was/were + verb-3 + by + O

Contoh :

Active : She wrote a letter.Passive : A letter was written by her.

C. Simple Future Tense

Active : S + will + verb-1 + O

Passive : S + will + be + verb-3 + by + O

Contoh :

Active : She will write a letter.Passive : A letter will be written by her.

D. Present Perfect Tense

Active : S + have/has + vetb-3 + O

Passive : S + have/has + been + verb-3 + by + O

Contoh :

Active : She has written a letter.Passive : A letter has been written by her.

E. Future Perfect Tense

Active : S + will + have + vetb-3 + O

Passive : S + will + have + been + verb-3 + by + O

Contoh :

Active : She will have written a letter.Passive : A letter will have been written by her.

Present continuous

Active : S + To be (am/are/is) + V-ing + O

Passive : S + To be (am/are/is) + being + V3 + by..

rumus passives:

present tense:

object + am/is/are + verb 3 + by + subject

past tense:

object + was/were + verb 3 + by + subject

present continuous:

object + am/is/are + being + verb 3 + by + subject

past continuous:

object + was/were + being + verb 3 + by + subject

future tense:

object + will + be + verb 3 + by + subject

present perfect:

object + have/has + been + verb 3 + by + subject
