15. Asha, Ben and Kemal shares $640 in the ratio 8 : 15 : 9. Ben spends 3/5 of his share on computer and 1/3 of the remaining on clothes. How much is left on his share?​


Ben's share that is left is $80.

More Explanation

Ratio is a relationship of two quantities, usually expressed as quotient of one divided by the other.

The ratio is usually expressed as a fraction.

Given:Total money of Ashan, Ben, and Kemal = $640ratio of each of their money = 8 : 15 : 9Ben spends 3/5 of his share on computerBen uses 1/3 of the remaining on clothesRequired:

Ben's share that is left = . . . . ?

Solution:

Given that Ben's share is 15 out of 32 ratio.

First, find the amount of Ben's share.

[tex]\tt Ben's\:\:share = \tt \dfrac {15}{8 + 15 + 9} \times 640[/tex]

[tex]\tt Ben's\:\:share = \tt \dfrac {15}{32} \times 640[/tex]

Ben's share = $300

Next, find the amount of share used on computer.

[tex]\tt amount\:\:of\:\:share\:\:used = \tt \dfrac {3}{5} \times 300[/tex]

amount of share used = $180.

So, the remaining share = $300 - $180 = $120

Next, find the [tex]\tt \dfrac {1}{3}[/tex] of remaining, which is $120, on clothes.

[tex]\tt amount\:\:of\:\:share\:\:used = \tt \dfrac {1}{3} \times 120[/tex]

amount of share used = $40

Amount of share left = $120 - $40 = $80

33. there were 50 stamps in an album. 30% of the stamps were local stamps. how many local stamps were there in the album? ​


Jawaban:

there are 15 local stamps in the album.

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

[tex]30\% \times 50 \\ = \frac{30}{100} \times 50 \\ = \frac{1500}{100} \\ = 15[/tex]

semoga membantu

Jumlah local stamps = (Persentase local stamps / 100) x Jumlah total local stamps

Diketahui bahwa persentase local stamps adalah 30% dan jumlah total stamps adalah 50. Oleh karena itu, kita dapat menempatkan nilai-nilai ini ke dalam rumus di atas:

Jumlah local stamps = (30 / 100) x 50

Jumlah local stamps = 0,3 x 50

Jumlah local stamps = 15

Dengan demikian, terdapat 15 local stamps di dalam album.

how to share your personal information appropriately?

Personal data can only be shared if there is a clear legal basis to do so or if the data subject has given their clear consent.

Personal information can be shared without consent if it is justified in the public interest or required by law. Do not delay disclosing information to obtain consent if that might put children or young people at risk of significant harm.
