5. 1.What the meaning of obligation and give some examples 2.What the meaning of prohibition and give some examples 3.What the meaning of suggestion / advice and give some examples 4what the meaning of command and give some examples


Jawaban:

Obligation ; something you HAVE to do. If you don't do it, there will be consequences such as punishments, fees, etc. Some obligations we know are praying, resprecting every human, help each other, etc.Prohibition ; something you SHOULD NOT do. If you do it, you will get punishments or else. Prohibitions we often know are talking down to parents or other people, make fun of people and make them sad, etc.Suggestion ; something you can do or not do. Some suggestions are like warnings. If we don't do it, bad thing may happen. Some suggestions we always heard are it is not recommended to eat late at night, it is better to plan things beforehand, etc.Command ; writings, sayings, or signs that tells prohibitions, obligations, or/and suggestion. We might also know them as rules. Some commands we know in daily life are "Don't take the bus at night!", "Please use hand sanitizer before using", "Watch your head!", etc

   announcement, it consist to inform the listeners or the readers about some infomation. 
   How about notice? The purpose of notice is to tell the people about the condition around them. e.g. No Trespassing!! Keep out from grass. Basicly the content of notice isn't to dangerous if we compare with caution content.
   Last, message. The function of message is, to inform the reader about some information too.... Announcement purpose is to announce public about something

Notice : is one of the functional text in form of guide in order to make people do or don't do something. Notice can be phrase, clause, or sign

Message : is a functional text that have a purpose to express something to other people to make them do or don't do something, message is created because the sender can't meet face to face with the receiver of the message. A message can be a memo
