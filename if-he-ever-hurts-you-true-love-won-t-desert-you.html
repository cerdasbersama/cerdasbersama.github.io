13. …… you give up easyly, you won´t achieve your ultimate goals.


Jawaban:

Conditionals:

If you give up easily, you won´t achieve your ultimate goals.

Penjelasan:

Jawaban dicetak tebal dan merupakan materi pembelajaran bentuk kalimat Conditionals (kalimat pengandaian) dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Jika kamu menyerah dengan mudah, kamu tidak akan mencapai tujuan akhirmu.

Kalimat pengandaian tipe 1

if+ subject + VI (Simple Present Tense) + Subject + will + Verb-1

atau

If+ subject + (be) present + adjective/noun + Subject + will be + adjective/noun

29. 1. Have you ever ___ (fly) a kite? (+) ______________________ (-) ______________________ 2. Have you ever ___ (do) bungee jumping? (+) ______________________ (-) ______________________ 3. Have you ever ___ (visit) Eiffel Tower? (+) ______________________ (-) ______________________ 4. Have you ever ___ (find) a four-leaf clover? (+) ______________________ (-) ______________________ 5. Have you ever ___ (ride) a camel? (+) ______________________ (-) ______________________ 6. Has he ever ___ (catch) a big fish? (+) ______________________ (-) ______________________


jawaban

1. fly = flown
(+) you ever have flown a kite
(-)you ever haven’t flown a kite

2. do = done
(+) you ever have done bungee jumping
(-) you ever haven’t done bungee jumping

3. visit = visited
(+) you ever have visited Eiffel Tower
(-)you ever haven’t visited Eiffel Tower

4. find = found
(+) you ever have found a four-leaf clover
(-)you ever haven’t found a four-leaf clover

5. ride = ridden
(+) you ever have ridden a camel
(-) you ever haven’t ridden a camel

6. catch = caught
(+) you ever have caught a big fish
(-) you ever haven’t caught a big fish


pembahasan

present perfect tense
(+) subject + have/has + v3
(-) subject + have/has + not + v3
(?) have/has + subject + v3 + ?

37. If you were thirsty, you will drink orange Jawab pertanyaan dengan TRUE (T) atau FALSE (F), lalu identifikasi jenis apa yang termasuk dalam kalimat bersyarat!


Jawaban:

False

Penjelasan:

If you were thirsty, you will drink orange

Kata will salah penempatannya karena itu adalah jenis kalimat Second Conditional yang memiliki formula:

If+S+V2, S+would/wouldn't+V1

kalimat yang benar seharusnya :

If you were thirsty, you would drink orange
