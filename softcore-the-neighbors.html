24. Complete the sentence with “was” /”were”!We ...................at the cinema last SundayMy neighbors ..................abroad last nightDiana ....................in Spain last weeke​


Jawaban:

We were at cinema last Sunday.

My neighbors were abroad last night.

Diana was in Spain last week.

Penjelasan:

Pemakaian was dan were selalu bergantung kepada subjek

Bila subjek yang ditunjuk adalah I, he, she, it, memakai was

Bila subjek yang ditunjuk adalah you, we, they memakai were

Jadi bisa dibilang subjek yang memakai was hanya ada 1 orang yang di tunjuk (kecuali "YOU" karena "YOU" memakai were)
