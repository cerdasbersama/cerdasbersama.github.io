What's the origin of the phrase 'Put the wood in the hole'?

This is a colloquial North of England expression of uncertain origin and date.

The 'wood' is the door and the expression is usually used when someone leaves a door open and lets in cold air to a warm room. Television dramas have given the term a wider audience but it is still largely confined to the North of England. Even there it is less used than before, probably due to the increased used of central heating which means there is less cause to use it.

31. Mrs. Yunita .... three daughter A. Have B. Makes C. Has D. Use


Jawaban:

C. Has

PEMBAHASAN Simple Present Tense

Digunakan untuk menyatakan Kegiatan yg dilakukan secara berulang, kebiasaan, rutin dan fakta.

Rumus Present Tense

(+) S + V1 (-s/es) + O/C

(-) S + do/does + not + V1 + O/C

(?) Do/does + S + V1 + O/C?

Catatan

Jika Subjek tunggal:

kalimat positive menggunakan Verb+s/es atau HASkalimat negative dan interrogative menggunakan does dan Verb-1 have

Jika Subjek jamak:

kalimat positive menyatakan Verb1 tanpa tambahan -s/es atau HAVEkalimat negative dan interrogative menggunakan do dan Verb-1 have

Pilihan yg tersedia

A. Have

Verb-1 untuk Subjek jamakArtinya mempunyai atau memiliki

B. Makes

Verb+s untuk Subjek tunggal Artinya membuat

C. Has

Untuk Subjek tunggal Artinya memiliki/mempunyai

D. Use

Verb-1 untuk Subjek jamakArtinya menggunakan

KESIMPULAN

Mrs. Yunita HAS three daughter

✔️ Subjek tunggal

✔️ maka menggunakan HAS
