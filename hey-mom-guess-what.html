B : come on, you make me curious

B : oh...yeah, Batik is traditional, but still elegant. I have ever seen a fashion model wearing a Batik in a fashion show. It's pretty good

A : what's gonna kid do in a fashion show like you ?

B : no way, i did rather choose being a fashion model than be it's designer
A : oh, you want to be famous ?
B : correct, one of my reason for being a model is because i want to be famous
A : i bet you able to reach your ambition
