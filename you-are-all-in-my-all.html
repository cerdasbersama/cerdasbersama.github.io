28. * MAKE THE SENTENCES INTO PASSIVE VOICE 1 . a : all the students are having obey the school rules p : 2. a : i borrow you my pen today p : 3. a : my headmaster gave many advices to all their students in school yard yesterday p : 4. a : anita will visit to her grandmother in sukabumi next moth p : SEKALIAN KASIH SENTENCES NYA YA PLSSSS


Jawaban:The school rules are being had obey by all the students you are borrowed my pen by me today Many advices were given by headmaster to all their students in school yard yesterdayHer grandmother will be visited by Anita in sukabumi next moth

Pembahasan

Active Voice

Kalimat aktifSubjek melakukan aksi atau perbuatan dalam kalimatnya

Passive Voice

Kalimat PasifObject dikenai aksi atau tindakan oleh subject

1. Present Continuous

a : S + Tobe (is/am/are) + V-ing + O

all the students are having obey the school rules

p : O + tobe (is/am/are) + being + V3 + By Subj

the school rules are being had obey by all the students

2. Present Tense

a : S + V1 (-s/es) + O

I borrow you my pen today

p : O + Tobe (is/am/are) + V3 + By Subj

you are borrowed my pen by me today

3. Past Tense

a : S + Verb 2 + O

my headmaster gave many advices to all their students in school yard yesterday

p : O + Tobe (was/were) + V3 + By Subj

many advices were given by headmaster to all their students in school yard yesterday

4. Future Tense

a : S + will + V1 + O

anita will visit her grandmother in sukabumi next moth

p : O + will be + V3 + By Subj

Her grandmother will be visited by Anita in sukabumi next moth
