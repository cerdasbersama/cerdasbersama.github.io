19. My mother is giving me a nice present ubah kedalam kalimat passive


Jawaban:

I am being given by my mother a nice present

Pembahasan

My mother is giving me a nice present

Structure Kalimatnya:Subject => My mother Tobe => isVerb-ing => Giving Object => meComplement => a nice present

Berdasarkan keterangan di atas:Kalimat bentuk aktif karena Subjek melakukan aksiKalimat bentuk Present Continuous karena menggunakan Tobe (is) + V-ing (Giving)

Berdasarkan Rumus:

Active: S + is/am/are + V-ing + O

=> My mother is giving me a nice present

Passive: O + is/am/are + being + V3 + By S

=> I am being given by my mother a nice present
