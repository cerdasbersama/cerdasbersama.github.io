7. Martin is ..... a good person that i'm sure you 'll be happy with him ​


Jawaban:

Martin is such a good person that I'm sure you'll be happy with him

Penjelasan:

Kalimat di atas menunjukkan pada seorang obyek yang memiliki sifat baik. Dimana kata sifat di atas dilalui dengan kata benda

Penggunaan such ... that

O + ROS + such + a/an + Adj +Noun + that + ROS

Contohnya:

She is such a smart girl that I'm so sure everyone will like herThis is such a hot day that I really want to eat an ice creamIf you ask me, I think Edgar get grounded again because he is such a lazy boy that he thinks homeworks are nothing for him

Penggunaan so ... that

O + ROS + so + Adj + that + ROS

Contohnya:

She is so smart that everyone will like herThis day is so hot that I really want to eat an ice creamIf you ask me, I think Edgar get grounded again because he is so lazy that he thinks homeworks are nothing for him

*ROS = Rest of sentence (bisa S/V/O/C)

Kalimat di atas melalui adjektiva (kata sifat) dan nomina (kata benda), jadi kata yang tepat adalah such

Penggunaan so ... that pada kalimat di atas

Martin is so good that I'm sure you'll be happy with him

Jadi, penggunaan so ... that tidak dilalui dengan noun tapi langsung ke adjective-nya

Penggunaan such ... that pada kalimat di atas

Martin is such a good person that I'm sure you'll be happy with him

Materi: Cause and Effect

Tingkatan: Sekolah Menengah Atas

Kata kunci: So ... that, Such ... that
