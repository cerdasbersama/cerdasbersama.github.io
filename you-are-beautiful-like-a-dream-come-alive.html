4. Example : your hair is beautiful. I like the color. 3. You are so gorgeous________ 4. You are so clever. __________


Answer:

The sentences in the exercise is related to expression of compliment and followed by the statement on the certain specific praise on something related of what is said in the compliment. Here's the answer:

3. You are so gorgeous. I love your sweet smile and lovely figure. (more related to physical beauty)

(other possible ones: I love your mesmerizing eyes.

                                  I really like the way you look at me. It's so alluring)

4. You are so clever. I am into your knowledge and profound thoughts. (more related to personality and the quality of being resourceful)

(other possible ones: I really fancy the way you present yourself to public.

                                   I adore your elegance in dealing with the conflict, very graceful.)

Hopefully it's quite sufficient.

20. Susunlah dibawah ini dengan benar beautiful-you-girl-what-you are-a


what a beautiful girl you are
semoga membantuAnswer:

The jumbled words above if you reorder it, it will become an expression of compliment. The pattern such expression is as follows:

What + adjective + noun + S + auxiliary verb! OR
How + adjective + S + auxiliary verb!

Here's the example of them:
- What a great goal it was!
- How great the goal was! 

So, the answer for the exercise above is 
What a beautiful girl you are!
