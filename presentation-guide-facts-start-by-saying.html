LABEL

ANIDAN

1. Brand of drug => ANIDAN  

2. Name of drug => Paracetamol tablets

3. Descriptions => Contains paracetamol

4. Content/Amount => Each tablet contains paracetamol Ph Eur 500mg

5. Use(s) => For relieve from:

Cold and flu symptoms  Feverishness  Aches and pains  Headache  Migreine  Toothache Period pain  Rheumatic pain  Neuralgia  Sore throat

6. Directions to use &dosage =>

Do not take with any other paracetamol containing products

Dosage:  

Adults, the elderly and young people aged 12 years of age or over: Take 2 tablets with a drink of water. Repeat every 4 hours as required.  DO NOT TAKE MORE THAN 8 TABLETS IN 24 HOURS.  Children 6 to 12 years of age: T ake ½ to 1 tablet with a drink of water. Repeat every 4 hours as required.  DO NOT TAKE MORE THAN 4 TABLETS IN 24 HOURS.  If symptoms persist for more than 3 days, consult your doctor.  DO NOT GIVE TO CHILDREN AGED UNDER 6 YEARS.  

7. Directions to the store => Keep all medicines out of the sight & reach of children

8. Expiration date => No date is available on the side of the packaging

Sina-Spritz

2. Name of drug => Instant Hand Degermer

3. Description of drug  => Kills 99,99% of E. coli, Salmonella enterica,  and Staphylococcus aureus (MRSA) in 15  seconds. Enhanced with moisturizers.
