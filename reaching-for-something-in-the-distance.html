28. A cyclist took 2 hours to cycle from the community centre to the beach,he cycled a distance of 132 km for another 3 hours from the beach to the bank.the distance between the community centre and the bank was 220 km a. find the speed of the cyclist for the distance from the beach to the bank b. what was the average speed for the whole distance ​


Jawab:

a. 44 km/jam

b. 44 km/jam

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Diketahui : t1 = 2 jam

                  s1 = 220 - 132 = 88 km

                  t2 = 3 jam

                  s2 = 132 km

Ditanya : a. V2 = ?

               b. V rata-rata = ?

a. V2 = s2 / t2 = 132 km / 3 jam = 44 km/jam

b.  V1 = s1 / t1 = 88 km / 2 jam = 44 km/jam

     V rata-rata = V1 + V2

                                2

                        = 44 + 44

                                2

                        = 44 km/jam

"Something are being said by My teacher in a class d. "Something have been said by My Teacher in a classe. "My teacher said something in a class​

Penjelasan:

penggunaan was/were dalam past tense.

Was - I/He/She/It

Were - You/They/We

- Dan meletakkan kata sebaliknya dalam peraturan Passive/Active Voice.

semoga membantu!!

Jawaban:

Multiple Choice:

b. "Something was said by My teacher in a class"

Penjelasan:

Jawaban dicetak tebal pilihan b. "Something was said by My teacher in a class"

Berdasarkan teks pada soal, bentuk kalimat aktif yang digunakan adalah Simple Past Tense karena menjelaskan aktivitas kegiatan di masa lampau dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Kalimat aktif subjeknya aktif melakukan aksi dalam kalimat.

Kalimat pasif subjeknya pasif dikenai aksi dalam kalimat.

Simple Past Tense:

Active Voice: (+) Subject + Verb-2 + Object

Passive Voice: (+) Subject + Tobe (was/were) + Verb-3 + Object
