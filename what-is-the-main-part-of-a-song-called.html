1. the part of the song that shifts is called


This is called Modulation which basically just means a planned key change, specifically "Sequential Modulation" meaning that it changes to the next key in a sequence, and not randomly, and even more specifically a "Diatonic Sequential Modulation" meaning that the sequence of key changes is diatonic.


This is not just common in electronic music, but in most forms of pop music as well, and at this point has become a trope known by some as the "Truck Driver's Gear Change", although it's mostly called that when it comes at the end of a song, just repeating the chorus over and over, modulating it each time.

1. The topic of the song is the life of Eva Perón, the first lady of Argentina.

2. The parts of the songs are Verse 1, Verse 2, Chorus, Verse 3, and Outro.

3. The main idea of each verse are:

Verse 1: The writer or singer couldn't express their feelings because nobody will believe them.Verse 2: The writer or singer had to let that happen.Chorus: The writer or singer's love of Argentina will never change.Verse 3: The writer or singer doesn't want to be rich or famous, as long as Argentina love them.Outro: The writer or singer need to prove to Argentina that their words are the truth.

Penjelasan:

Song atau lagu merupakan komposisi musik dari kata-kata, ayat, atau puisi yang dinyanyikan untuk mengekspresikan perasaan.

Lagu dapat digunakan untuk mengekspresikan emosi, perasaan, serta kritik terhadap situasi masyarakat. Moral value atau amanat biasanya tersirat dalam lirik yang dibuat penulis lagu.

Lagu memiliki language features sebagai berikut:

Imagery, digunakan untuk menggambarkan perasaan yang dirasakan si penulis.Rhythm, digunakan untuk menciptakan suasana dalam lagu.Figures of speech, digunakan untuk mencari tahu mengenai arti dalam lagu.

Lagu juga memiliki generic structure, diantaranya:

Intro, bagian awal lagu dan biasanya hanya instrumennya saja.Verse, bagian pengantar ke chorus.Refrain, bagian peralihan dari verse ke chorus.Chorus, bagian inti lagu yang paling mudah diingat.Bridge, bagian penghubung chorus ke chorus selanjutnya atau ke coda.Outro atau Coda, bagian penutup lagu.

Definition of Song

A song is a single (and often standalone) work of music intended to be sung by the human voice with distinct and fixed pitches and patterns using sound and silence and a variety of forms that often include the repetition of sections.

"Lagu adalah karya musik tunggal (dan seringkali mandiri) yang dimaksudkan untuk dinyanyikan oleh suara manusia dengan nada dan pola yang berbeda menggunakan suara dan keheningan dan berbagai bentuk yang sering termasuk pengulangan bagian"

Social Function of Songs

The social function of song are:

To entertain the listeners.  (Untuk menghibur para pendengar)To teach moral value through the lyrics of the songs.  (Untuk mengajarkan nilai moral melalui lirik lagu)To provide a way of managing the relationship between our public and private emotional life.  (Untuk menyediakan cara mengelola hubungan antara kehidupan emosional publik dan pribadi kita)To express personal feeling and cultural values.  (Untuk mengekspresikan perasaan pribadi dan nilai-nilai budaya)To give someone to not only shares their emotions with others, but also to have an emotional connection that just can’t be experienced in any other way.  (Memberi seseorang untuk tidak hanya berbagi emosinya dengan orang lain, tetapi juga untuk memiliki hubungan emosional yang tidak bisa dialami dengan cara lain)
