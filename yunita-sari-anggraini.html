[tex] uang \: fahri \: = \frac{5}{11} \times 99.000 \\ =rp. 45.000 \\ \\ uang \: yunita \: = \frac{6}{11} \times 99.000 = \\ = rp.54.000[/tex]

Uang Fahri = Rp 45.000,00Uang Yunita = Rp 54.000,00

Uang Fahri

[tex] = \frac{5}{6 + 5} \times 99.000[/tex]

[tex] = \frac{5}{11} \times 99.000[/tex]

Uang Yunita

[tex] = \frac{6}{6 + 5} \times 99.000[/tex]

[tex] = \frac{6}{11} \times 99.000[/tex]

[tex] = 54.000[/tex]
