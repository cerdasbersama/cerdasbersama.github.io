15. Rere : What are you looking for?Sendy : I'm.......my key. I can'tenter my room.A. Looking forB. looked forC. looks forD. look for​


Jawabannya adalah..

(A) Looking for

PEMBAHASAN

Karena setelah subject "I" terdapat to be "am" maka kata kerja yang digunakan harus verb-ing. Dimana ini menunjukan bahwa kalimat tersebut merupakan contoh dari present continuous.

Present continuous adalah tenses yang menjelaskan suatu perbuatan yang sedang berlangsung pada masa sekarang.

Rumus :

(+) S + To be (am/are/is) + V1-ing + O

(-) S + To be (am/are/is) + not + V1-ing + O

(?) To be (am/are/is) + S + V1-ing + O

Contoh :

(+) They are watching TV.

(-) They are not watching TV.

(?) Are they watching TV?

Tanda waktu :

At the momentNowRight nowEtc

Jadi, jawaban yang tepat untuk melengkapi dialog di atas adalah (A) Looking for.

18. Make questions for the answers using a second conditional What / you / do / if / you / win / lots of money


Answer:

Second conditional is the conditional form in English which states the unreal or imaginary situation. Such won't happen in real or in the future. Here's the pattern

S + would + V1 + complement, If + past tense

Example: I wouldn't see that movie, if I knew it was bad.

If the car exploded, we would die.

The question from the prompt; what / you / do if you / win / lots of money would be like this below

What would you do if you won lots of money?

Hopefully that's clear enough.

Kelas: SMP

Mapel: English

Kategori: second conditional
