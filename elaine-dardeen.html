3. Jane will hoover the sitting room if Aaron and Tim move the furniture.

5. Elaine will buy the drinks if somebody helps her carry the bottles

EXPLANATION

Conditional type 1 [First conditional]

➡ Keinginan yang kemungkinan terjadi saat ini/mendatang berdasarkan prediksi (real possibility).

Rumus

If+Simple present, Simple future

If+S+verb¹(s/es)..., S+will+verb¹....

Exp.

If she brings the swimsuit, she will swim in the pool.

Conditional type 2 [Second conditional]

➡ Keinginan yang diragukan terjadi saat ini/mendatang (hypothetical condition).

Subjective pronoun seperti : he, she dan it menggunakan to be(were) sbg bentuk subjunctive.

Rumus :

If+Simple past, Past future

If+S+verb2 ......, S+would+verb1......

Exp.

If I were you, I would do anything to catch my dream.

Conditional tipe 3 [Third conditional]

➡ Keinginan yang tidak terwujud di waktu lampau.

Pengulasannya saat ini hanyalah sebagai dampak dari kejadian di waktu lampau yg tdk terpenuhi.

Rumus :

If+Past perfect, Past future perfect

If+S+had+V³..., S+would+have+V³.....

Exp.

If her mother had permitted her, she would have been happy.
