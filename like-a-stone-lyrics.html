The elements of the song lyrics are divided into two, namely:

(1) The elements of form that can be referred to as physical structures, include among others:

- diction

- figuratively

- Pengimajian

- concrete words

- rhythm

- as well as typography.

(2) The elements of content or inner structure consist of:

- theme

- feelings

- tone

- and mandate.

Penjelasan:

“They built a new high stone wall around the garden.” = “Mereka membuat sebuah dinding batu tinggi yang mengelilingi taman.”

E. Dindingnya terbuat dari batu tinggi.
