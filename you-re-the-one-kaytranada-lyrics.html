Jawaban:

The purpose of the lyrics in "Safe and Sound" by Taylor Swift feat. The Civil Wars is to provide a sense of comfort, reassurance, and safety. The lyrics evoke a feeling of protection and solace in challenging or uncertain times. They convey a message of support and resilience, assuring the listener that they will be safe and protected.
