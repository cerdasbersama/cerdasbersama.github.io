20. Total Weight = 3000 lbs Weight shifted = ? Distance shifted = 50 inches ΔCG = 8 inches


Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Given that the total weight is 3000 lbs, the distance shifted is 50 inches, and ΔCG is 8 inches, we can substitute these values into the formula:

Weight Shifted = 3000 * (8 / 50)

Weight Shifted = 480 lbs

Therefore, the weight shifted due to an 8-inch change in the center of gravity over a distance of 50 inches is

480 lbs.


21. A runner covers a distance of 35 km in 6 hours and 20 minutes. Find the average speed in(a) kilometers per hour(b) centimeters per second Write your answer in 3 significant figures.


average speed = total distance / total time

(a) To find the average speed in kilometers per hour:

total distance = 35 km

total time = 6 hours and 20 minutes = 6.33 hours (since 1 hour = 60 minutes)

average speed = 35 km / 6.33 hours = 5.53 km/h

Therefore, the average speed in kilometers per hour is 5.53 km/h.

(b) To find the average speed in centimeters per second, we need to convert kilometers per hour to centimeters per second. We can use the following conversion factors:

1 km = 1000 m

1 m = 100 cm

1 hour = 3600 seconds

So, 1 km/h = (1000 m / 1 km) * (100 cm / 1 m) / (3600 seconds / 1 hour) = 27.78 cm/s

Using this conversion factor, we can convert the average speed in kilometers per hour to centimeters per second:

average speed = 5.53 km/h * 27.78 cm/s = 153.27 cm/s

Therefore, the average speed in centimeters per second is 153.27 cm/s

27. Spiral Review James is building a rectangular fire pit. He dug a hole 20 inches by 20 inches. He decides to make each side of the fire pit 4 inches longer instead. What is the percent increase in the area of the fire pit?


Jawaban:

The original area of the fire pit is the product of its length and width:

Area = 20 inches x 20 inches = 400 square inches

After James increases each side of the fire pit by 4 inches, the new length and width become 20 + 4 = 24 inches. Therefore, the new area is:

New Area = 24 inches x 24 inches = 576 square inches

To find the percent increase in area, we need to calculate the difference between the new area and the original area, divide by the original area, and then multiply by 100 to convert the answer to a percentage:

Percent Increase = [(New Area - Original Area) / Original Area] x 100%

Substituting the values we have, we get:

Percent Increase = [(576 - 400) / 400] x 100%

Percent Increase = (176 / 400) x 100%

Percent Increase = 0.44 x 100%

Percent Increase = 44%

Therefore, the percent increase in the area of the fire pit is 44%.beri bintangnya kakak

Area of Frame and Picture

Area = L * W

L = 9 

W = 6

Area = 9*6 = 54 in²

Area of Picture

Area = L*W

L = 6

W = 4

Area = 6 * 4

Area = 24 in²

Area of Frame

Area of Frame = Total Area - Picture Area

Area of Frame = 54 - 24

Area of Frame = 30 in²
