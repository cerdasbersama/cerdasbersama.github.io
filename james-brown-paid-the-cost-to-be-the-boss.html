13. To produce 1 unit of a new product, a company determines that the cost for material is $2.50 and the cost of labor is $4. The fixed cost, regardless of sales volume, is $5000. If the cost to a wholesaler is $7.40 per unit, determine the least number of units that must be sold by the company to realize a profit​


Jawaban:

The cost for material is $2.50.

The cost of labor is $4.

The fixed cost, regardless of sales volume, is $5000.

If the cost to a wholesaler is $7.40 per unit, the least number of units that must be sold by the company to realize a profit is 677 unit

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Given:

The cost for material is $2.50.

The cost of labor is $4.

The fixed cost, regardless of sales volume, is $5000.

The cost to a wholesaler is $7.40 per unit

To Find:

The least number of units that must be sold by the company to realize a profit is ...?

Answer:

Total Cost = The cost for material + The cost of labor + The fixed cost

Total Cost = $2.50 + $4 + $5000

Total Cost = $5006,5

The cost to a wholesaler =  

The least number of units = Total Cost / The cost to a wholesaler

The least number of units = $5006,5 / $7.40 = 676,5 = 677 unit.

The least number of units = 677 unit

So, the least number of units that must be sold by the company to realize a profit is 677 unit.

40. Chase, Mafia and TimeCold decided to contribute some money to buy a present for their mother. Chase agreed to contribute 30% of the cost of the present while Mafia agreed to contribute 40% of the remaining amount. The rest of the price of the present will be paid by TimeCold. However, on the shopping day, they realised the price of the item had increased by 25%. Chase paid $36 for his share in the end. a) What was the original price of the present? b) How much did TimeCold have to pay for the present in the end?


Jawab:

A.) 96$

B.) 36$

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Chase contributes 30%

30% = 36$

[tex]x[/tex] = 36$ [tex]: \frac{30}{100}[/tex]

[tex]x[/tex] = 36$ × [tex]\frac{100}{30}[/tex]

[tex]x[/tex] = [tex]\frac{3600}{30}[/tex]

[tex]x[/tex] = [tex]120[/tex]

([tex]x[/tex] is a scale)

Mafia contributes 40%

40% = ?$

40% × [tex]x[/tex] = ?$

[tex]\frac{40}{100}[/tex] × 120 = ?$

[tex]\frac{4800}{100}[/tex] = ?$

[tex]48[/tex] = ?$

40% = 48$

TimeCold paid the rest of the price (100% - 30% - 40%)

TimeCold contributes 30%

30% = 36$

The price of the present = 36$ + 36$ + 48$

                                         = 120$

So, to find the original price, we must divide increased price with the total percent of the increased price (100% + 25% = 125%)
