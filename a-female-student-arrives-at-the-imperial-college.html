PEMBAHASAN

Tense disini adalah simple present tense.

Simple present tense digunakan untuk menyatakan kegiatan rutin, kebiasaan, dan hal yang bersifat umum (pengetahuan umum/fakta)

STRUKTUR KALIMAT

Struktur kalimat disini adalah untuk kalimat verbal.

→ HE, SHE, IT

Subyek he, she, it menggunakan akhiran 's' atau 'es'. Subyek he, she, it menggunakan 'does'.

BERIKUT STRUKTUR KALIMATNYA:

(+) Subject + verb 1 + '-s/es'  

Contoh: He drinks a glass of water.    

(-) Subject + does not + verb 1    

Contoh: He doesn't drink a glass of water.    

(?) Does + subject + verb 1    

Contoh: Does he drink a glass of water?

→ THEY, WE, I, YOU

Subjek they, we, i, you tidak menggunakan akhiran 's' atau 'es'. Subjek they, we, i, you menggunakan 'do'.

BERIKUT STRUKTUR KALIMATNYA:

(+) Subject + verb 1    

Contoh: They play basketball.    

(-) Subject + do not + verb 1    

Contoh: They don't play basketball.  

(?) Do + Subject + verb 1  

Contoh: Do they play basketball?

35. 4 A student goes on a 800 kilometres long journey by train. The train leaves at 12.30 and arrives at the destination at 17.30. Calculate the average speed of the train. Include units with your answer.​


To calculate the average speed of the train, we use the formula:

Average Speed = Total Distance / Total Time

Total Distance = 800 km
Total Time = (17 hours and 30 minutes) - (12 hours and 30 minutes) = 5 hours

Now, we convert 5 hours into minutes:
5 hours x 60 minutes/hour = 300 minutes

Average Speed = 800 km / 300 minutes ≈ 2.67 km/minute (rounded to two decimal places)

So, the average speed of the train is approximately 2.67 kilometers per minute.
