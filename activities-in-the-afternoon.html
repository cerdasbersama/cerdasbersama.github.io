23. five activities in the morning and afternoon and night and evening


Jawaban:

The five possible activities in the morning, afternoon, evening and night indicate the daily routine. Here are they

Morning activities:

getting uptaking a bathhaving breakfastgoing to school/workdoing exercise

Afternoon activities:

working/ studying at schoolhaving lunchtaking a language/ music coursehaving a meetingtaking a nap

Evening activities:

going home from workhaving dinnerwatching movies/ TVs at homehanging out at the cafedoing some homework

Night activities:

reading before going to bedsleepingprayingwatching football matchesplaying games on mobile phone

Penjelasan:

The activities mentioned above are the typical ones on each part of the day. Even though those are daily routines, they are not written in sentences but in words as they are more focusing on the activities instead of someone daily routines. Thus, the gerund is used.

Gerund is a noun which is formed by verb. The verb is added by suffix -ing to make a gerund, which normally tells about hobbies or activities. Here are the example in the sentence:

- Getting up early hasn't been easy for some people. (activities)

- They love playing football. (hobbies)

Jawaban:

(+) The children sleep in the afternoon

(-) The children don't sleep in the afternoon

(?) Do the children sleep in the afternoon?

Penjelasan:Simple Present Tense adalah salah satu dari bentuk tenses yaitu tenses yang menyatakan sesuatu kebiasaan setiap hari (sehari-hari), situasi yang tidak berubah, dll.

Untuk membuat kalimat simple present tense,kita harus gunakan kata dasar dari kata kerja/verb: (I take, you take, we take, they take). Bentuk orang ketiga tunggal menggunakan -s di akhir kata dasar. (he takes, she takes).

Rumus :

● Positip (+) = Subjek + Verb 1 (s/es) + Objek

I go to school every morning.She reads book everyday.They watch horror movies every friday.

● Negatip (-) = Subjek + Do/does + not + verb 1 + Objek

I don't go to school every morning.She doesn't read book everyday.They don't watch horror movies every friday.

● Interogatip (?) = Do/Does + Subjek + Verb 1 + Objek

Do i go to school every morning?Does she read book everyday?Do they watch horror movies every friday?

Catatan:Untuk subjek i, you, we, they tidak perlu menggunakan -ies, -es atau -s di akhiran kata kerja (verb 1).Sedangkan untuk subjek he, she, it harus menggunakan -ies, -es, -s di akhir kata kerja (verb1).

Kesimpulan:subjek the children merupakan subjek yang bersifat jamak atau lebih dari satu orang maka harus menggunakan "do" jika negatif (don'T) yaitu singkatan dari do not.keterangan waktu nya atau time signal nya merupakan keterangan waktu dari simple present tense yaitu "in the afternoon"

(+) The children sleep in the afternoon

(-) The children don't sleep in the afternoon

(?) Do the children sleep in the afternoon?
