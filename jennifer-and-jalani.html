10. Dahlia: Jennifer, would you like to give me an opinion about my poetry? Jennifer: Of course! I think you should use attractive themes and titles. It would be great poetry. Dahlia: Thank you very much, Jennifer. Jennifer: It’s okay. The dialogue above tells us that Dahlia is… * a. Make a wish b. Asking for sing a song c. Waiting for help d. Sharing an opinion e. Asking for an opinion


☁️QUESTION(PERTANYAAN):

Dahlia: Jennifer, would you like to give me an opinion about my poetry?

Jennifer: Of course! I think you should use attractive themes and titles. It would be great poetry.

Dahlia: Thank you very much, Jennifer.

Jennifer: It’s okay.

The dialogue above tells us that Dahlia is… *

a. Make a wish

b. Asking for sing a song

c. Waiting for help

d. Sharing an opinion

e. Asking for an opinion

☁️ANSWER(JAWABAN):

e. Asking for an opinion (Menanyakan pendapat)

☁️EXPLANATION(PENJELASAN):

Dahlia: Jennifer, would you like to give me an opinion about my poetry? (Jeniifer, Bolehkan kau memberikanku pendapat tentang puisi ku?)

Jennifer: Of course! I think you should use attractive themes and titles. It would be great poetry. (Tentu saja! Menurutku kamu harus menggunakan tema dan judul yang menarik. Itu akan menjadi puisi yang bagus)

Dahlia: Thank you very much, Jennifer. (Terima kasih banyak, Jennifer)

Jennifer: It’s okay. (Tidak apa-apa)

The dialogue above tells us that Dahlia is… (Dialog diatas memberi tahu kita bahwa Dahlia...)

a. Make a wish (membuat permohonan)

b. Asking for sing a song (meminta menyanyikan lagu)

c. Waiting for help (menunggu bantuan)

d. Sharing an opinion (berbagi pendapat)

e. Asking for an opinion (menanyakan pendapat)

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Nomor 28

Total = 25 siswa

Gemar IPA = 17 siswa

Gemar Matematika = 10 siswa

Gemar keduanya = 8 siswa

Tidak gemar keduanya

= (17 + 10 + 8) - 25

= 35 - 25

= 10 siswa

Nomor 29

Gemar bulu tangkis = 20 anak

Gemar tenis meja = 15 anak

Gemar keduanya = 12 anak

Jumlah (total) anak

= (20 - 12 ÷ 2) + (15 - 12 ÷ 2)

= (20 - 6) + (15 - 6)

= 14 + 9

= 25 anak

Jawaban:

Modal

= 275.000+25.000 =300.000

Penjualan

(3.500 × 25) ÷ ¼

= 350.000

Nilai keuntungan

= Penjualan/Modal

= 350.000 ÷ 300.000

= 16,67% (untung)

Jawabannya B
