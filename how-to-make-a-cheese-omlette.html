8. cara buat how to make cheese cake


how to make the perfect cheesecake.

1) Use a springform pan and a water bath. The springform pan works best because it lets you remove the cake easily and the water bath prevents unsightly cracking on the top of your cheesecake.

2) When mixing ingredients, mix evenly to ensure that all lumps in the cream cheese are eliminated before adding the eggs. Eggs generally bring air in the batter, so add them last and stir as little as possible once the eggs have been added.

3) Do not overbeat the cake batter. Once the ingredients are mixed well, stop stirring.

4) Bake the cheesecake at the recommended oven temperature using a water bath. To avoid water seepage, wrap the springform cake pan in foil about halfway up the side of the springform pan. Set the springform pan wrapped in foil in a larger pan containing hot water. The level of the water should not be higher than the foil around the springform pan.

5) Watch your baking time carefully and avoid over-baking your cheesecake. There are several successful methods for determining when to remove the cheesecake from the oven: 1.) When it is slightly moist in the center, 2.) When it has reached an ideal temperature of 160-165 degrees or, 3.) When a small knife inserted in the center comes out clean. If the cheesecake is about done and you are worried about over cooking it, you may turn off the oven and let it sit in the oven for a little while longer to ensure that the cake sets completely.

6) After removing the cheesecake from the oven, run a knife around the edge of the cheesecake. This helps to loosen the cake from the edge of the pan, allowing it to pull away freely as it cools.

7) Allow the cheesecake to cool completely. The cooling step is as important as proper mixing and baking. You should cool the cheesecake at room temperature, away from drafts. Once the cheesecake has cooled completely you should place the cheesecake (pan and all) in the refrigerator for at least 6 hours or overnight. Once it’s thoroughly cooled, it will be much easier to remove the springform pan and it is ready to serve to your guests.

17. Kuis[+50]: (+) The baker has run out of cheese used to make cheese bread. (-) (?)


Penjelasan:

(-) The baker hasn't run out of cheese used to make cheese bread yet.

(?) Has the baker run out of cheese used to make cheese bread yet?

Susunan kalimat untuk kalimat negatif present perfect (secara umum) :

Subject + has / have + V3 + (bisa ada object, keterangan) + yet. (tidak ada yet juga gapapa)

Susunan kalimat untuk kalimat tanya present perfect (secara umum) :

Has / have + Subject + V3 + (bisa ada object, keterangan) + yet?

Has dipakai untuk subject : "He, she, it"

Have dipakai untuk subject : "I, you, we, they"

Semoga membantu!

Jawaban:

(+) The baker has run out of cheese used to make cheese bread

(-) The baker hasn't run out of cheese used to make cheese bread.

(?) Has the baker run out of cheese used to make cheese bread?

________________________

Pendahuluan:

Present Perfect Tense adalah sebuah tense yang digunakan untuk menyatakan sesuatu yang dikerjakan di masa lampau yang telah selesai di masa itu namun dampak atau efeknya masih terasa hingga masa kini.

Rumus:

(+) S + has/have + V3 + O

(-) S + has/have + not + V3 + O

(?) Has/Have + S + V3 + O + ?

dengan:

Has 》 He, She, It

Have 》 I, You, We, They

****************************************

Pembahasan Soal:

Kalimat di soal menggunakan Present Perfect Tense. Maka untuk merubah kalimat tsb menjadi bentuk (-) dan (?), gunakan rumus yg telah tersedia di atas.

~ Untuk kalimat negatif, hanya tinggal menambahkan not setelah has. Maka menjadi:

(-) The baker has not run out of cheese used to make cheese bread.

Has not bisa disingkat menjadi hasn't. Maka, boleh dibuat menjadi "has not" ataupun "hasn't".

~ Untuk kalimat interogatif:

Bisa dilihat dari formula, bahwa "has/have" nya terletak di depan. Maka, tinggal pindahkan has (krn kalimat soal menggunakan has) ke depan, menjadi:

(?) Has the baker run out of cheese used to make cheese bread?

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Kesimpulan:

Jadi, bentuk negatif dan interogatif dari kalimat di soal adalah:

(-) The baker hasn't run out of cheese used to make cheese bread.

(?) Has the baker run out of cheese used to make cheese bread?

26. arrange the correct steps to make a cheese omelet! please gak tau cara buat nya


1. Crack and put the eggs in a bowl and beat well until uniform yellow color.(usually, for every 3 eggs, put 2 yolks, more yolks means more cholesterol).
2. Add a pinch of salt.
3. Heat a pan and add vegetable oil or butter (butter being more fattening).
4. Get the omelette ingredients ready and hand-toss in a separate bowl.
5. Pour egg into pan and let cook for about 7 seconds (the hotter the pan, the shorter you wait), do not fold the egg or touch the egg with a spatula during the 7 seconds.
6. Pour omelette contents into center of still liquid egg part.
7. Fold the "edges" of the egg over onto itself to cook the cheese and other contents. After about 20 seconds of total cooking time, use the spatula and partially dice the contents and stir-fry like normal.
8. Take pan off once the egg has turned solid, but before it becomes browned.
9. Serve hot

Beat together the eggs and some salt and pepper in a bowl.Put an 8-inch nonstick skillet over medium-high heat and wait a minute. Add the butter or oil; when the butter melts, swirl it around the pan until its foam subsides, then pour in half of the egg mixture to cover the bottom of the pan.Sprinkle half the cheese evenly over top and cook until just the sides are firm, then use a rubber spatula to fold over one edge about 2 inches. Goal : To Make a Cheese Banana
Tujuan : Untuk membuat Pisang Keju

Ingredients / Materials ( Bahan - bahan )
6 medium-sized bananas, peeled
( 6 pisang berukuran sedang yang sudah dikupas )
3 tbsp rice flour
( 3 sendok teh tepung beras )
3 tbsp wheat flour
( 3 sendok teh tepung gandum )
1/2 tsp vanilla essence
( 1/2 sendok teh perasa vanilla )
2 tbsp milk creamer
( 2 sendok teh krimer susu )
Grated cheese
( Keju yang sudah diparut )
Oil for deep-frying
( Minyak untuk menggoreng )
A pinch of salt
( Sejumput garam )
A pinch of sugar
( Sejumput gula )

Steps ( Langkah - langkah ) :
1.First, mix sugar, rice flour and wheat flour with water.
( Pertama, campur gula, tepung beras dan tepung gandum dengan air )
2.Then soak bananas in the batter. Heat the oil in a large wok.
( Kemudian, rendam pisang dalam mentega. Panaskan api di wajan yang besar )
3.Then drop one banana at a time into the oil. Cook only two bananas at a time as the temperature of the oil will be drop if there are too many bananas.
( Kemudian, masukkan satu persatu pisang ke dalam minyak. Masak cukup dua pisang saja dalam satu waktu karena kalau ada terlalu banyak pisang, suhu minyaknya akan menurun drastis )
4.When bananas are crisp and golden brown, remove from oil and drain on a paper towel and put aside.
( Ketika pisangnya sudah garing dan berwarna cokelat keemasan, angkat dari minyak, keringkan di tisu dan letakkan di tempat lain )
5.Pour the milk creamer on top of the fried bananas.
( Tuang krimer susu di atas pisang yang sudah masak )
6.Finally, coat it with cheese.
( Terakhir, lapisi pisang itu dengan keju )
