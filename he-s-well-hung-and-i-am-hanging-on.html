1. He prefers helping his father to ……. with his friends. a. hang out c. hung e. is hanging b. hanging out d. will hang


Jawaban:

A. Hang out

Penjelasan:

Hang out = pergi keluar

hung = v3 hang = menggantung

is hanging = gerund hang = menggantung

hanging out = gerund hang out

will hang = akan menggangung

kemungkinan antara hang out dan hanging out, tapi karena ada to, jadi to+infinitve, tidak boleh gerund. jadi hang out
