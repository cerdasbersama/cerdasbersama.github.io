9. The sun ... When the climber reached mount everest (shine )


The sun is shining when the climber reached mount everest
(kalo gasalah ini)

Subject : English

Category : Tenses (Past Simple and Past Continuous)

Level : JHS VIII

Keywords : when, while


Past Simple Tense describes an event that happened and completed in the past.

Pattern : S + Preterite (v2)


Past continuous tense describes an event that was happening around a specific time in the past.

Pattern : S + was/were + present participle


Past continuous and past simple are often used together. The past continuous describes the longer or background action while the past simple describes the action that interrupts the background action.



ANSWERS :

The sun WAS SHINING when the climber reached Mount Everest.
