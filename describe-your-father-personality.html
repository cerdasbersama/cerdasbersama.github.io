28. Tolong di Jawab dong contoh Dari Describe your friends orally. Include their age, weight,height,hair,and personality


"Describe your friend(s) orally", maksudnya kita ditugaskan untuk menjelaskan teman atau teman-teman kita dengan cara berbicara (orally).

Contoh Describing their Age ﻿(Umur): "He is 16 years old, a year older than me"
Contoh Describing their Weight ﻿(Berat badan): "His weight is 60 Kilograms"
Contoh Describing their Height ﻿(Tinggi badan): "His height is around 160 centimeters"
Contoh Describing their Hair ﻿(Rambut): "His hair is short and black-colored"
Contoh Describing their Personality ﻿(Sifat/Perilaku): "He is a good friend, kind, and thoughtful"

Nah, kalau mau jadi text describing untuk berbicara, tinggal di gabung deh.
