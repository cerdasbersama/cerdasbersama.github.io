16. Please help me tonight, I need your answer, thank you for your help ​


Diketahui:

U3 = a + 2b = 9

U5 + U7 = 36

Ditanyakan:

U10 = ... ?

Penyelesaian:

U5 + U7

= a + 4b + a + 6b

= 2a + 10b = 36

a + 2b = 9 ... (1) kali 2

2a + 10b = 36 ... (2) kali 1

2a + 4b = 18

2a + 10b = 36

_______________ -

- 6b = - 18

b = 3

Subtitusikan ke pers 1

a + 2b = 9

a + 2 (3) = 9

a + 6 = 9

a = 3

U10 = a + 9b

U10 = 3 + 3(3)

U10 = 3 + 9

U10 = 12

==================

Detil Jawaban

Kelas: 9

Mapel: Matematika

Bab: Barisan dan Deret Bilangan

Kode: 9.2.2

Kata Kunci: Barisan aritmatika

U3 = 9

a + (n-1).b = 9

a + (3-1).b = 9

a + 2b = 9 ... (1)

U5 + U7 = 36

a + 4b + a + 6b = 36

2a + 10b = 36 ... (2)

Eliminasi persamaan (1) dan (2)

a + 2b = 9

2a + 10b = 36

(Yg atas dikalikan 2, sedangkan yg bawah dikalikan 1 agar ada yg sama)

2a + 4b = 18

2a + 10b = 36

___________ -

-6b = -18

b = 3

Substitusikan :

a + 2b = 9

a + 2(3) = 9

a + 6 = 9

a = 9-6

a = 3

Mencari U10 :

U10 = a + (n-1).b

= 3 + (10-1).3

= 3 + 9.3

= 3 + 27

= 30

Jadi, nilai U10 adalah 30
