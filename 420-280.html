8. If a car factory checks 280 cars and 7 of them have defects, how many will have defects out of 420?.


Jawaban:

If a car factory checks 280 cars and 7 of them have defects, the proportion of cars with defects is 7/280 = 1/40. If the factory checks 420 cars, the number of cars with defects will be (1/40)*420 = <<(1/40)*420=10.5>>10.5 cars. Since it is not possible for there to be half a car, the final answer is that 10 cars out of 420 will have defects.
