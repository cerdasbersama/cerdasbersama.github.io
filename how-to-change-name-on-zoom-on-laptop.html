21. Bagaimana cara check laptop sudah on berapa lama dari baru?


Pada Windows 8

Buka Windows task manager dengan mengklik kanan pada taskbar lalu pilih Task manager, atau anda juga bisa membukanya dengan menekan tombol CTRL + SHIFT + ESC.

Setelah task manager pilih tab Performance, lalu anda bisa melihat pada Up time, dan itu adalah waktu lamanya komputer anda sudah menyala.

Pada Windows 7/XP/Vista

Buka Windows task manager dengan mengklik kanan pada taskbar lalu pilih Start Task manager, atau anda juga bisa membukanya dengan menekan tombol CTRL + SHIFT + ESC.

referensi gambar: http://indradp-share.blogspot.com/2014/10/cara-mengetahui-waktu-lama-komputer-menyala.html

29. change the sentence based on its form!(+)I wen to pekalongan last month (-)______(?)____​


Jawaban:

(+) I went to Pekalongan last month.

(-) I didn't go to Pekalongan last month.

(?) Did I go to Pekalonga last month.​

Penjelasan:

Pada past tense, kalimat negatif dan tanya, kata kerjanya berbentuk verb 1.

(+) subject + verb 2

(-) Subject + didn't + verb 1

(?) Did + subject + verb 1

Jawaban:

(+) I went to Pekalongan last month

(-) I did not go to pekalongan last month

(?) Did I go to pekalongan last month ?

Penjelasan :

Simple Past Tense

Simple past tense adalah kalimat tenses yang bertujuan untuk mengungkapkan kejadian di masa lalu.

Pattern :

a. Verbal

(+) Subject + Verb 2 + Object/Compliment + Adverb of time

(-) Subject + Did + Not + Verb 1 + Object/Compliment + Adverb of time

(?) Did + Subject + Verb 1 + Object/Compliment + Adverb of time + ?

b. NonVerbal

(+) Subject + Was/Were + Adj/Noun/Adv

(-) Subject + Was/Were + Not + Adj/Noun/Adv

(?) Was/Were + Subject + Adj/Noun/Adv + ?

Note :

a. I,She,He,It : was

b. You, We, They : were

Adverb of Time Simple Past :

1. Last month

2. Last Year

3. Years ago

4. Month ago

5. Last week ago

6. Etc...

38. Name : Procedure Text Class: Write a procedure text based on the pictures below! How To Make Fried Tapioca Flour (Cireng)​


Jawaban:

1. Puree the garlic and thinly slice the spring onions first, then set them aside in 1 container

2. Add tapioca flour and add salt. Stir until evenly mixed with the garlic and spring onions.

3. Boil water until it boils. Then, take 2 tablespoons of the flour mixture and put it in boiled water. Stir until the water turns thick like glue.

4. Next, mix the starter into the remaining tapioca flour mixture. Stir until evenly mixed using a spoon or hands until textured

5. Shape the dough into a round shape first, then flatten it. However, avoid flattening it too thin because then your flakes will become hard. Repeat rounding all the dough until you run out.

6. Heat oil in a frying pan and fry the cireng until yellowish white and crispy.
