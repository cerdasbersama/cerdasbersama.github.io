A greeting card is is a short text in the form of a message consisting of prayer and hope. Greeting cards are usually sent to someone who is celebrating an occurrence or is experiencing an exceptional event.

Receiver: To: Mr.David / Dear Mr.DavidBody: The purpose of the body / content is to congratulate, pray, hope, and also show sympathy. The purpose of this greeting card depends on the situation of the recipient of the card. Like when sending a greeting card to a friend who is celebrating his/her birthday, the goal is to share Happiness.Sender: It is the sender of greeting cards. - Memiliki tujuan / identitas penerima.
