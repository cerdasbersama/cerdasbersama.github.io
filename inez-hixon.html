Positive

Subject (he/she/it) + verb 1(+s or es)

Subject (he/she/it) does not +verb 1 +object + adverbial time

Subject (I/you/they/we) + do not + verb 1+ object + adverbial time

Introgative

Do +subject (I/yoy/they/we)+verb 1+object + adverbial time
