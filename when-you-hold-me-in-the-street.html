23. What do you have to do……..? What mustn’t you do ………..? 1. in campus 2. as a student 3. as a close friend 4. when you meet a stranger 5. when you talk to your parents 6. when you cross the street 7. in a cinema 8. when you play game in your Smartphone 9. in an ATM 10. in a gas station 11. in the central market


1. In campus:

- Do attend classes regularly.

- Don't engage in disruptive behavior or vandalism.

- Do respect the rules and regulations of the campus.

- Don't plagiarize or cheat in exams.

2. As a student:

- Do prioritize your studies and assignments.

- Don't procrastinate or leave things until the last minute.

- Do actively participate in class and ask questions.

- Don't engage in academic dishonesty, such as copying someone else's work.

3. As a close friend:

- Do listen and support your friend when they need someone to talk to.

- Don't betray their trust or spread their secrets.

- Do be there for them in both good and bad times.

- Don't manipulate or take advantage of their friendship.

4. When you meet a stranger:

- Do be polite and friendly.

- Don't overshare personal information or trust blindly.

- Do maintain personal boundaries and be cautious.

- Don't engage in risky or unsafe situations.

5. When you talk to your parents:

- Do communicate openly and respectfully.

- Don't lie or hide important information from them.

- Do express gratitude and appreciation for their support.

- Don't disrespect or argue with them unnecessarily.

6. When you cross the street:

- Do obey traffic rules and signals.

- Don't jaywalk or cross at undesignated areas.

- Do look both ways for incoming traffic.

- Don't use electronic devices or be distracted while crossing.

7. In a cinema:

- Do respect others by remaining quiet during the movie.

- Don't use your phone or any electronic devices that may disturb others.

- Do dispose of trash properly and keep the area clean.

- Don't talk loudly or engage in disruptive behavior.

8. When you play games on your smartphone:

- Do moderate your screen time and prioritize other responsibilities.

- Don't neglect your duties or relationships due to excessive gaming.

- Do respect the privacy and boundaries of other players.

- Don't engage in toxic behavior or harassment towards others.

9. In an ATM:

- Do protect your PIN number and shield your transactions.

- Don't share your PIN or disclose sensitive information to strangers.

- Do be aware of your surroundings and ensure the ATM is secure.

- Don't leave your transaction incomplete or forget to take your card.

10. In a gas station:

- Do follow safety guidelines and regulations.

- Don't smoke or use open flames near fuel pumps.

- Do pay attention while fueling your vehicle to prevent spills.

- Don't leave the gas pump unattended or block access for others.

11. In the central market:

- Do respect the vendors and their merchandise.

- Don't engage in theft or shoplifting.

- Do haggle politely if bargaining is culturally accepted.

- Don't create unnecessary chaos or disrupt the market's flow.
