Jawaban:

Pembahasan

Pola Past Perfect:

(+) S + had + V3 + Complement

(-) S + had not + V3 + Complement

(?) had + S + V3 + Complement

Keterangan pilihan ganda:

1. Past Continuous

(+) S + Tobe (was/were) + V-ing + O

=> I was sleeping when somebody knocked the door.

Penjelasan:

Opsi ini salah bukan Past Perfect

A. Was + Verb 2

=> I was slept when somebody knocked the door.

Penjelasan:

Was + V2 = salah

B. Past Perfect

(+) S + had + V3 + Complement

=> I had slept when somebody knocked the door.

Penjelasan:

C dan D. Present Perfect

(+) S + has/have + V3 + Complement

C. I have slept

D. I have slept

Penjelasan:

Ini kalimat Present Perfect, sedangkan yg diminta di soal adalah Past Perfect

Kesimpulan

Yang sesuai pola Past Perfect adalah B.

8. 1. Tom opens the door. The passive is ...A. The door was opened by TomB. The door opened by TomC. The door is opened by TomD. The door is being opened by TomE. The door opens by Tom​


The passive is “The door is opened by Tom”Jawabannya yang C ✔️

PEMBAHASAN

A. (+) S + Be (was/were) + V3 + By

➡️ The door was opened by Tom

Keterangan: Subject => The doorBe => WasVerb-3 => opened By agent => TomPenjelasan:Opsi A tidak tepat karena kalimat soal adalah Active-Present Tense Sedangkan Opsi A adalah kalimat Passive dari Past Tense karena menggunakan Be Past (was) dan Verb-3 (opened)

B. (+) S + Verb-2 + O

➡️ The door opened by Tom

Keterangan: Subject => The door Verb-2 => opened By TomPenjelasan:Opsi B tidak tepat karena secara pola memang salah. Dan kesalahan lainnya menggunakan Verb-2 setelah Subjek Sedangkan Pola Passive adalah Be+Verb-3.

C. (+) S + Be (is/am/are) + V3 + By

➡️ The door is opened by Tom

Keterangan:Subject => The doorBe => isVerb-3 => opened By agent => TomPenjelasan:Opsi C adalah yg TEPAT karena memiliki pola Be Present (is) dan Verb-3 (opened)

D. (+) S + is/am/are + being + V3 + By

➡️ The door is being opened by Tom

Keterangan: Subject => The door Be => isBeing + Verb-3 => being opened By agent => TomPenjelasan:Opsi C tidak tepat karena menggunakan Passive-Present Continuous “Be (is) Being + V3” setelah subject Sedangkan Pola Passive-Present adalah Be Present +Verb-3.

E. (+) S + Verb+s + O

➡️ The door opens by Tom

Keterangan: Subject => The door Verb+s => opens By TomPenjelasan:Opsi E tidak tepat karena secara pola memang salah. Dan kesalahan lainnya menggunakan Verb+s setelah Subjek Sedangkan Pola Passive adalah Be+Verb-3.

KESIMPULAN Active-Present Tense

(+) S + Verb-1+s + O

➡️ Tom opens the door.

Passive-Present Tense

(+) S + Be (is/am/are) + V3 + By

➡️ The door is opened by Tom

14. 1. The opens the door 2. Mommy cleans the room everyday 3. My uncle fixed the toy yesterday 4. Jono has taken a Mandarin course 5. Tono meets them everyday 6. Susi waters this plant every two day 7. He met them yesterday 8. He will meet then tomorrow 9. He would have met thame 10. He had met them before I came 1-10 tersebut dibuat kalimat pasive voice


PASSIVE VOICE

1. The door is opened by him

2. The room is cleaned by mommy everyday

3. The toy was fixed by my uncle yesterday

4. A Mandarin course has been taken by Jono

5. They are met by Tono everyday

6. This plant is watered by Susi twice a day

7. They were met by him yesterday

8. They will be met by him tomorrow

9. They would have been met by him

10. They had been met by him before I came

EXPLANATION

Passive voice, menitikberatkan pada aksi dan obyek (si penerima aksi) sebagai pemberitaan utama.

Obyek dalam kalimat active berubah posisi menjadi subyek dalam kalimat passive. Subyek (si pelaku aksi) dalam kalimat active berubah posisi diletakkan sebagai obyek preposisi (by Agent) dalam kalimat passive dan atau bahkan tidak dimunculkan.

Pola utama passive voice adalah penggunaan Be+Verb3.

ELABORATION

1. Simple Present

Active : S+verb1(s/es)+OHe opens the doorPassive : S+is/am/are+verb 3 + by AgentThe door is opened by himNote :

The door => a singular noun

2. Simple Present

Active : S+verb 1 (s/es)+OMommy cleans the room everydayPassive : S+is/am/are+verb 3 +by AgentThe room is cleaned by Mommy everyday

3. Simple Past

Active : S+verb2+OMy uncle fixed the toy yesterdayPassive : S+was/were+verb 3 +by AgentThe toy was fixed by my uncle yesterdayNote :

The toy => a singular noun

4. Present Perfect

Active : S+has/have+verb 3+OJono has taken a Mandarin coursePassive : S+has/have+been+verb 3+by AgentA mandarin course has been taken by JonoNote :

A mandarin course => A singular noun

5. Simple Present

Active : S+verb 1 (s/es)+OTono meets them everydayPassive : S+is/am/are+verb 3 +by AgentThey are met by Tono everydayNote :

They => a plural noun

6. Simple Present

Active : S+verb 1 (s/es) + OSusi waters this plant twice a day.Passive : S+is/am/are+verb 3 + by AgentThis plant is watered by Susi twice a day.Note :

This plant => a singular noun

7. Simple Past

Active : S+verb 2 + OHe met them yesterdayPassive : S+was/were+verb 3 +by AgentThey were met by him yesterdayNote :

They => a plural noun

8. Simple Future

Active : S+will+verb 1 + OHe will meet them tomorrowPassive : S+will+be +verb 3 + by AgentThey will be met by him tomorrow

9. Past Future Perfect

Active : S+would have +verb 3 + OHe would have met themPassive : S+would have+been+verb 3 + by AgentThey would have been met by him

10. Past Perfect

Active  : S+had+verb 3 + OHe had met them before I camePassive : S+had+been+verb 3+ by AgentThey had been met by him before I came.
