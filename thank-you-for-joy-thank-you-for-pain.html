Offering a help expressions adalah ungkapan menawarkan bantuan kepada orang lain untuk melakukan sesuatu, seperti menggunakan frase "Do you want me to ... ?"

Arrange the sentences into a good dialog!

Susunlah kalimat ini menjadi dialog yang baik!

Lucas : Good afternoon, Samuel.

Samuel : Good afternoon, Lucas.

Lucas : You look so pale. What's the matter?

Samuel : I don't know. My stomach is in pain.

Lucas : Did you eat something bad?

Samuel : I guess so, but I can't recall?​

Lucas: Do you want me to call the teacher?

Samuel : That's a kind of you, Lucas. Thank you so much.

Lucas : Never mind. Wait for a minute here. I'll call him. See you soon.

Respon yang diberikan:

That's a kind of you, Lucas. Thank you so much.
