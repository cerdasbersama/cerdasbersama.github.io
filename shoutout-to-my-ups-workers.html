"in oder to" artinya adalah untuk, agar atau supaya.makanya dikalimatnya Constructions workers need to use safety gears in order to keep themselves safety.Yang artinya,petugas konstruksi perlu memakai perlengkapan keselamatan supaya aman

Constructions workers need to use safety gears so that they can keep themselves safety.
