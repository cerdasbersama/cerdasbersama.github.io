Besides, I [2. be]to the toilet many times.jawablah dengan mengubah kata didalam kurung dengan present perfect tense atau past tense penjelasannya juga yah.​


Penjelasan:

Dian : Be optimistic. You just need to take a deep breath and stay focused.

Raka : I have taken a deep breath, but I still feel a bit nervous. Besides, I am.

Explanation:

"Take" is changed to "have taken" in the present perfect tense to indicate that the action of taking a deep breath has been completed and is still relevant in the present moment.

"Be" is left unchanged as it is already in the present tense, indicating the current state of being nervous.

23. 2 kamu belajar matematika setiap senin translate: you study math every monday.(+):(-):(?):​


Jawaban:

(+) You study maths every Monday.

(-) You don't study maths every Monday.

(?) Do you study maths every Monday?

Penjelasan:

Simple present tense =

used to denote an activity done as a daily/weekly habit

Rules:

(+) I/you/we/they + verb (transitive) + object

(-) I/you/we/they + don't + verb (transitive) + object

(?) Do + I/you/we/they + verb (transitive) + object?

-

(+) She/he/it + verb (transitive) -s + object

(-) She/he/it + doesn't + verb (transitive) + object

(?) Does + she/he/it + verb (transitive) + object?

<(7o7)>

Jawaban:

(+) You study math every Monday.

(-) You do not study math every Monday.

(?) Do You study math every Monday?

Penjelasan:

Jawaban dicetak tebal dan merupakan materi pembelajaran bentuk kalimat Simple Present Tense karena menjelaskan aktivitas kegiatan berulang.

Simple Present Tense:

Kalimat Positive (+)  :

Verbal     :  Subject + Verb1/Verb1+s/es + Object + adverb of time (every day / every morning)

Kalimat Negatif (-)    :

Verbal     :  Subject + do/does not +Verb1 + Object + adverb of time (every day / every morning)

Kalimat Interrogative (?)  :

Verbal    :  Do/Does + Subject + Verb1 + Object + adverb of time (every day / every morning) + ?
