7. Doreen makes a fruit drink. She uses apple juice, pear juice and watermelon juice in the ratio 5:3:7 respectively. She uses 2.8 litres of watermelon juice. (i) How much apple juice does she use?(ii) How much fruit drink does she makealtogether?​


Jawab:

i). 2 litres

ii). 6 litres

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Ratio Apple : Pear : Watermelon = 5:3:7

Ratio Volume = 5+3+7 = 10

Ratio Apple/Volume Ratio = 5/10

Ratio Pear/Volume Ratio = 3/10

Ratio Watermelon/Volume Ratio = 7/10

Volume Watermelon = 2.8 litres

i) Apple Juice used = 5/7 x 2.8 = 2 litres

ii) Pear Juice used = 3/7 x 2.8 = 1,2 litres

Fruit drink she make altogether = Apple + Pear + Watermelon

= 2 + 1.2 + 2.8

= 6 litres
