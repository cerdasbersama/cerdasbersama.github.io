26. 2. "Do you like this?". "Yes" The indirect speech of this dialogue is a. He asked me if I liked it so did I say yes b. He wondered whether I liked it and I say yes c. He wanted to know if I liked it and I sais yes I did d. He asked me if I liked it and I said I did e. He asked me if I liked it and I said I did​


Jawaban:

The correct answer to this question is C. "He wanted to know if I liked it and I said yes I did." In this dialogue, the direct speech is "Do you like this?" and the indirect speech is "He wanted to know if I liked it and I said yes I did.

Penjelasan:

The other options are either incorrect or incomplete. For example, option A "He asked me if I liked it so did I say yes" is missing the second part of the sentence, "and I say yes I did"; option B "He wondered whether I liked it and I say yes" is grammatically incorrect; option D "He asked me if I liked it and I said I did" is also grammatically incorrect; option E "He asked me if I liked it and I said I did" is incomplete because it is missing the second part of the sentence
