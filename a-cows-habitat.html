1. A farmer owns a total of 23 chickens and cows. The chickens and cows have 78 legs altogether. How many cows are there?


Jawaban:

Answer+Explain:

Diket.:

- Sapi + Ayam = 23

- Jumlah kaki sapi + ayam = 78

Misalkan:

Ayam = 2X

Sapi = 4Y

Sehingga:

X + Y = 23

2X + 4Y = 78

X = 23 - Y

2(23 - Y) + 4(Y) = 78

46 -2Y + 4Y = 78

46 + 2Y = 78

2Y = 32

Y = 16

Jumlah sapi = 16

Jumlah ayam = 23 - 16 = 7
