10. the Betawi What does the bengkong do? Mention the entertainment durin​


Penjelasan:

The Betawi people have a traditional performance called "bengkong" in their culture. The bengkong is a form of street performance or street theater that is usually performed during special occasions and celebrations. It involves a group of performers who dress in colorful costumes and portray various characters and stories through dance, music, and comedy.

During the bengkong performance, the performers entertain the audience with their lively movements, humorous dialogues, and traditional music accompaniment. They often portray everyday life situations, historical events, or folklore stories, using exaggerated gestures and facial expressions to engage the spectators.

The bengkong serves as a source of entertainment and amusement for both the Betawi community and visitors. It not only showcases the artistic talents of the performers but also preserves the cultural heritage and traditions of the Betawi people. The lively and vibrant atmosphere created by the bengkong performance adds to the festive spirit of the occasions it is performed in.
