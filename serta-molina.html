Expression yang digunakan pada tiap kalimat adalah expression of thanking. Expression ini digunakan untuk mengucapkan terima kasih. sebelum membuat dialog, kita sebaiknya mengetahui ungkapan yang dapat digunakan untuk mengekspresikannya terlebihd dahulu. berikut contohnya.

- thank you

- thanks

- thanks a lot

- thank you very much

- thanks for ...

- I'm very grateful for ...

1. Nadia has won a singing contest ellie congratulates her then, nadia says thanks

Ellie: Congratulations on winning the singing competition, Nadia! You are amazing!

Nadia: Thank you so much, Ellie.


2. A new neighbor asked where the park is Edo shows him the directions to the neighbor

The new neighbor: Excuse me. Can you help me?

Edo: Sure. What can i help you?

The new neighbor: Can you tell me where the park is?

Edo: Sure. First. walking down this street, then turn left. Go straight, until you see a mosque. The park is beside the mosque.

The new neighbor: I see. Thanks for your help

Edo: You're welcome

Budi's mother: sure

Dad: Really? let me see the bag.

Molina: Here it is.

Dad: Don't worry. I can stitch it for you.
