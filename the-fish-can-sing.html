16. imagine you are the fish in the story. can you narrate the story from his point of view? (Storynya berjudul The enchanted Fish) (Tolonggg saya)


Kelas: SMA
Pelajaran: English
Kategori: Narrative
Kata kunci: The Enchanted Fish, the fish's point of view
Pembahasan:

On a one fine day, I was swimming in the sea and wondering how I could transform back into a human being. Suddenly, I saw some kind of worm in the middle of the sea. 'Hmm, it looks delicious', I thought. 'though I prefer a roasted chicken in my old castle.' So, off I went to catch it. Unfortunately, it turned out to be a bait of a fisherman. I was powerless and pulled out by him. Yet, I refused to end up in the kitchen or worse, at the market, as I was originally a prince. Therefore, I spoke to him about my true nature and begged to let me go. He was definitely startled with what he saw. He must have thought that he had gone mad when he looked at me, a talking fish. Surprisingly, he did release me.
       On the next day, while I was strolling in the sea, I saw him again, but this time he intentionally called on me. He then asked if I could grant his wife's wish to have a nice cottage. Well, I felt pity on him but I owed him, so I did grant that wish with my magical power.
       After a while, I was surprised to see that fisherman on the sea. It turned out that he had another wish from his wife, being an emperor. I could see that he actually did not want it but he was so in love with his wife, with a bit of fear of her as well. I thought, 'Dude, don't let her control you. This had better be the last wish'. I was going to say it but I didn't have a heart. So, again, I granted it.
       Unfortunately, he did not learn the lesson. He came up again, this time in the middle of the storm. I said to myself, 'What on earth are you doing, man?'. That fisherman's wife had a ridiculous request this time, being the lord of Sun and moon. I think this had gone beyond the line. I had no choice but turning their life back to small hut, like they used to be.
