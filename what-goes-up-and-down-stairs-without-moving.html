Present Tense :

Digunakan untuk menyatakan hal yang terjadi sekarang.Menyatakan hal yang menjadi rutinitas.Menyatakan hal yang merupakan "truth fact"

Formula :

- Posotif :

S + Infinitif (V¹) + O

S = Subjek

V¹ = Verb ( Kata kerja dasar )

O = Objek

- Negatif

S + do/does + not + V¹ + O

do = untuk I,you,they,we

does = untuk she,he,it

- Kalimat tanya :

Do/does + S + V¹ + O + ?

Jika 5W + H :

What/Where/When/Who/Why/How + Do/Does + S + V¹ + O + ?

Di soal menggunakan formula yang kelimat tanya, maka setela Subjek,harus V¹ (kata kerja dasar),yaitu get up.

Karena :

gets up itu get + s ,digunakan dalam kalimat positif dan digunakan oleh subjek he,she,it.getting up , digunakan dalam present continous/progressive tense.goes : go + es, digunakan dalam kalimat positif dan digunakan oleh subjek he,she,it tapi ini out of topic karena ini go(pergi) bukan get up(bangun).

40. what is the difference between moving animals and plants


The majority of animals usually move their whole bodies often supported by specialised organs such as fins, wings and legs. These are called locomotory organs moving the animal from place to place. Plant movement is not locomotory and does not generally involve moving the whole body.

Jawaban:

While both plants and animals are living organisms but they can be differentiated with the movement characteristics. Plants can't move from one place to another while on the other hand animals can move from one place to another because animals contain specialized protein to promote movement in the body.
