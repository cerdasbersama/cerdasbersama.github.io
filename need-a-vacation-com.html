26. ======================================================1. I have enough money.=> _________________2. I need a long vacation.=> _________________3. We play soccer after school.=> _________________======================================================​


Jawaban:

1. I don't have enough money .

2. I don't need a long vacation.

3. We don't play soccer after school.

Penjelasan:

Jika subjeknya I , We , You , They atau kata benda jamak dengan kata kerja selain kata kerja be , Gunakan don't (Do not) didepan kata kerja untuk membuat kalimat negatif. Jika subjeknya He , She , It , atau kata benda tunggal dengan kata kerja selain kata kerja be , gunakan doesn't (Does not) didepan kata kerja untuk membuat kalimat negatif . Selain itu kata kerja yang digunakan adalah bentuk dasarnya tanpa menambahkan -s atau -es di akhir katanya.

Contoh soalnya:

1. I go to school by bus.

=> I don't go to school by bus.

2. He goes to the gym.

=> He doesn't go to the gym.

3. My mom works downtown.

=> My mom doesn't work downtown.
