CONDITIONAL SENTENCES

1. My laptop will work well if the battery is full charge.

2. If you close your eyes, you won't see anything.

3. If we have good character, everyone will like us.

4. Tiara would date you if you got much inheritance.

5. Desy would buy many expensive sport cars if she won the lottery.

6. If Corona didn't exist, the world would keep in peace.

7. If they had trained hard before, they would have won the match.

8. Kelly would have cooked the soup if she hadn't slept in the living room.

9. If we had published this, we would have been popular.

10. If my laptop had had high-end features, I would have downloaded all the big size game.

EXPLANATION

Conditional sentence adalah kalimat pengandaian, di mana suatu keinginan dapat terwujud jika persyaratannya terpenuhi.

Conditional type I

Keinginan yang kemungkinan terwujud saat ini berdasarkan prediksi. True in the present/future

Pola :

[Simple present + Future simple]

If+S+verb1(s/es)..., S+will+verb1......

Exp.

My laptop will work well if the battery is full charge.

Conditional type II

Keinginan yang diragukan terwujud saat ini tetapi belum pasti benar. Untrue in the present/future

Pola :

[Simple past + Past future]

If+S+verb²......, S+would+verb¹......

Exp.

Tiara would date you if you got much inheritance.

Conditional type III

Keinginan yang sangat tidak mungkin terwujud di waktu lampau. Pengulasannya saat ini hanyalah gagasan yang kemungkinan membawa perubahan . Untrue in the past/Truly hypothetical

Pola :

[Past perfect + Past future perfect]

If+S+had+Verb³ ..., S+would+have+verb³..

Exp.

If my laptop had had high-end features, I would have downloaded all the big size game.

Procedure Text

The translation of the text:

Connect the charger to your hand phone, the flash symbol on the charger plug must face upward. (Sambungkan pengisi daya ke hand phone Anda, simbol flash pada steker pengisi harus menghadap ke atas) Wait until the battery icon appears on the screen. (Tunggu sampai ikon baterai muncul di layar) Charge the battery approximately 5 hours or until the battery icon indicates that the battery is fully charged. (Isi daya baterai sekitar 5 jam atau hingga ikon menunjukkan bahwa baterai telah terisi penuh) Remove the charger by pulling out from your hand phone. (Lepaskan pengisi baterai dengan menarik keluar dari handphone Anda)

PEMBAHASAN:

Teks mengenai cara mengisi baterai handphone di atas merupakan jenis procedure text. Tujuan dari procedure text adalah untuk memberitahu pembaca bagaimana melakukan atau membuat sesuatu melalui urutan tindakan atau langkah-langkah.  

Generic Structure of Procedure Text  

Goal: memberikan informasi tentang maksud dan tujuan prosedur dan memprediksi suatu kesimpulan. (provides information about the purpose and objectives of the procedure and predicts a conclusion)  Materials: berisi daftar materi atau bahan-bahan yang dibutuhkan untuk melakukan suatu prosedur atau langkah-langkah. (contains a list of materials or materials needed to carry out a procedure or steps)  Steps: daftar urutan instruksi/aktivitas untuk mencapai tujuan dalam urutan langkah yang benar. (list the sequence of instructions / activities to reach the destination in the correct sequence of steps)  

Language features of Procedure Text  

Mengunakan Simple Present Tense (S+V1)  Berbentuk imperative/ bentuk perintah,  Menggunakan action verbs, contoh: make, take, boil, cook,  Menggunakan temporal conjunctions, contoh: First, second, third, then, next, after that, last and finally

24. The circuit diagram shows a 4.0 ω resistor and an 8.0ω resistor connected to a 6.0v battery. what is the current in the battery ?


Based on the circuit diagram, when two resistors connected in a series form, the total value of the resistance is the sum of all the resistor. So the total resistance of the resistors is 12 Ohm.

The next step is to calculate the current which flows to the circuit, based on the formula to find the current which is the total voltage divided by the total resistance, hence 6 divided by 12 which is 0.5 Ampere.
