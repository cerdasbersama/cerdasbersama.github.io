34. Student's Exercise Task 1 1. Your friend has a problem to wake up early in the morning. Answer: You should sleep early 2. Your friend wants to get her mother a birthday present, but she doesn't have enough money. ............ 3. Your friend wants to get better score in maths. ........... 4. Your friend always forget where she puts her purse. .......... 5. Your friend wants to get a new pair of shoes, but they are very expensive. 6. Your friend wants to be accepted in a good high school...........​


Berikut ini adalah jawaban untuk soal-soal tersebut

1. You should sleep early

2. I suggest her to save her money.

3. He ought to study more.

4. He should put her purse in a visible place so that it is easy to find.

5. He should save his money or find cheaper ones.

6. They ought to study more and achieve a good score.

Pembahasan

Soal tersebut meminta kita untuk memberikan saran atas situasi yang diberikan. Untuk menyatakan saran (suggestion) dalam bahasa Inggris, ada beberapa frasa yang dapat digunakan, diantaranya:

1. Should ..

2. Ought to...

3. I suggest you to ...

4. My suggestion is ...

5. I advise that ...
