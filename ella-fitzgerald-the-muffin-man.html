19. rosita membuat muffin cokelat dan muffin strawberry. untuk membuat 18 muffin cokelat memerlukan 4 kg terigu, dan untuk membuat 15 muffin strawberry memerlukan 3 kg terigu. berapa kg terigu yang diperlukan, jika rosita akan membuat 216 muffin cokelat dan 315 muffin strawberry?​


Jawaban:

Jawabannya:

Diketahui:

Muffin Cokelat

18 Muffin = 4 Kg Terigu

Muffin Strawberry

15 Muffin = 3 Kg Terigu

Pertanyaan:

Sebanyak 216 Muffin Cokelat & 315 Muffin Strawberry akan dibuat, berapa Kg Tepung Terigu yang dibutuhkan?

Jawabannya:

Muffin Cokelat:

216 ÷ 18 = 12

12 × 4 = 48 Kg Terigu

Muffin Strawberry:

315 ÷ 15 = 21

21 × 3 = 63 Kg Terigu

Jawaban:

• 18 roti muffin cokelat = 4kg terigu

untuk 216 roti berisi muffin cokelat dibutuhkan

216/18 × 4 = 48kg terigu

• 15 roti muffin strawberry = 3kg terigu

untuk 135 roti isi muffin strawberry dibutuhkan

135/15 = 27kg tepung terigu

jadi jumlah terigu yang dibutuhkan adalah

48kg + 27kg

= 75kg

36. ELLA:Anna:Ella:Anna:Ella:Daniel:Anna:Ella:Daniel:Ella:Daniel:​


Jawaban:

Once upon a time, in a quaint little village, there were three best friends: Ella, Anna, and Daniel. They were inseparable, always seeking adventures and fun together.

One sunny morning, as they gathered near the old oak tree in the village square, Ella excitedly announced, "Let's go on a treasure hunt! I heard a rumor that there's a hidden treasure in the abandoned castle on the outskirts of town."

Anna, always up for a challenge, replied, "That sounds thrilling! Count me in. But we must be careful; the castle is said to be haunted."

With determination in their eyes, the trio set out on their quest. The journey was filled with laughter, camaraderie, and a touch of nervousness as they approached the eerie castle. As they stepped inside, the creaky floors and eerie echoes sent shivers down their spines.

Ella bravely led the way, following a tattered map she had found. They encountered puzzling traps and riddles that they solved together as a team. Anna's sharp wit, Daniel's strength, and Ella's resourcefulness complemented each other perfectly.

After what felt like an eternity, they reached the heart of the castle where the legendary treasure was rumored to be hidden. But to their surprise, there was no chest full of gold and jewels. Instead, they found a worn-out diary belonging to a forgotten adventurer.

Intrigued, they gathered around as Ella started reading the diary aloud. It revealed the story of a kind-hearted knight who, centuries ago, had ventured into the castle seeking a fabled gem that granted wishes. The knight had selflessly used the gem to help others in need, leaving no trace of the treasure behind.

Disappointed about the treasure, the friends realized that the real treasure was the bond they shared and the unforgettable memories they had created during the adventure.

Daniel smiled and said, "Perhaps the true treasure was the journey itself, the friendships we've forged, and the lessons we've learned."

Anna nodded in agreement, "You're right. Our bond is worth more than any material wealth."

Ella added, "And who knows, maybe our willingness to help others, just like that noble knight, will lead us to our own treasures in life."

From that day on, Ella, Anna, and Daniel continued to explore the world together, supporting each other in every step of their journey. They learned that true treasures could not always be measured in gold but in the cherished moments and the people they held dear. And so, their adventures continued, making their story one of friendship, courage, and the pursuit of the extraordinary in the ordinary.


37. apa deskripsi dari muffin?


Muffin merupakan kue khas negeri Inggris dan lahir pada zaman Victoria. Pada saat itu muffin banyak dijual oleh pedagang keliling dengan diletakkan di nampan di atas kepala. Muffin merupakan sejenis makanan tradisional berbentuk gulungan, bundar dan tipis. Bahan dasarnya terbuat dari adonan roti yang diberi ragi. Paling enak, kue manis ini dinikmati saat musim dingin dan disajikan bersama minuman hangat seperti teh atau kopi.
