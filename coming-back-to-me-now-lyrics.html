13. present continous kalimat passive my teacher is coming to the meeting now​


active: my teacher is coming to the meeting now ​(guru saya sedang datang ke pertemuan sekarang)

passive: the meeting is being come by my teacher now (pertemuan itu sedang didatangi oleh guru saya sekarang)

________

passive voice present continuous:ingat ini rumusnya:

S + To be (is/am/are) + being + verb 3 + by + O

the meeting + is + being + come + by + my teacher now

verb1 -- verb 2 -- verb 3

come -- came -- come

__

contoh lain:

active: I am doing my homework right now (aku sedang mengerjakan PR ku sekarang)

passive: S + To be (is/am/are) + being + verb 3 + by + O

my homework + is + being + done + by + me right now

my homework is being done by me right now (PR ku sedang dikerjakan oleh ku sekarang)

__

verb1 -- verb 2 -- verb 3

do -- did -- done
