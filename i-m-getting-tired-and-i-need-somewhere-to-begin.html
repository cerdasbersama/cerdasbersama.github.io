Menggunakan Simple Present Tense

Digunakan untuk menyatakan kegiatan atau keadaan

➪ Yang dilakukan berulang-ulang

➪ Yang terjadi sekarang

Rumus

(+) S + Verb (s/es) + O

(-) S + Don't/Doesn't + Verb (Base form) + O

(?) Do/Does + S + Verb (Base form) + O

Catatan

Subyek She, He, It

➪ Untuk kalimat positif verb ditambah

dengan (s/es)

➪ Untuk kalimat negatif menggunakan

doesn't

➪ Untuk kalimat pertanyaan menggunakan

does

Subyek I, We, They, You

➪ Untuk kalimat positif verb tidak ditambah

dengan (s/es)

➪ Untuk kalimat negatif menggunakan

don't

➪ Untuk kalimat pertanyaan menggunakan

do

29. What is the difference in meaning between these pairs of setences! A. I am tired of working under pressure and i am tired from working hard. B. I stopped eating and i stopped to eat. C. I remember writing a latter to my Mom and i remebered to write a latter mom to my mom Tolong dibantu ya


What is the difference in meaning between these pairs of sentences!

A. I am tired of working under pressure and I am tired from working hard.

B. I stopped eating and I stopped to eat.

C. I remember writing a letter to my mom and I remembered to write a letter to my mom.

-----------

Answers

I am tired of working under pressure.

I am being pressured while working and I am annoyed by it. (Saya kesal karena saya bekerja dibawah tekanan)

I am tired from working hard.

I am exhausted because I have been working hard. (Saya lelah karena saya habis bekerja keras)

I stopped eating.

I was eating then I stopped. (Saya berhenti makan)

I stopped to eat.

I was doing something then I stopped doing it to eat. (Saya berhenti melakukan sesuatu untuk makan)

I remember writing a letter to my mom.

I remembered that I had written a letter to my mom. (Saya ingat saya sudah menulis surat kepada ibu saya)

I remembered to write a letter to my Mom.

I remembered that I hadn't written a letter to my mom and I should write to her soon. (Saya ingat untuk menulis surat. Suratnya belum ditulis).

------------

Expanation

A

be tired of something = annoyed or bored with something

tired from something = exhausted because of doing something

B and C

gerund vs. to+infinitive

gerund → something is being done or has been done (sedang atau sudah terjadi)

to+infinitve → something is not done yet (belum terjadi)
